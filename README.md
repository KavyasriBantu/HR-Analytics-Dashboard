
# HR Analytics Dashboard

This Tableau dashboard provides insights into employee attrition, demographics, and job satisfaction within the organization.

DashboardLink:https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/kavyasri.bantu/viz/HRDashboard_17255254632590/HRDashboard

Key Metrics

Total Employees: 961 
Attrition Count: 133  
Attrition Rate: 13.84%  
Active Employees: 828  
Average Age: 37

Dashboard Sections

1.Department-Wise Attrition:
Sales department has the highest attrition (56.12%), followed by R&D (38.82%), and HR (5.06%).  
2.Employee Count by Age Group:
Most employees fall between the ages of 27-33, with a peak at 30 years old (134 employees).        
3.Attrition by Gender:
Male attrition: 90 (67.67%)
Female attrition: 43 (32.33%)  
4.Education Field-Wise Attrition:
Life Sciences sees the highest attrition (59), followed by Medical (47).  
5.Job Satisfaction by Role:
Satisfaction levels by job role are visualized in a heatmap, with Research Scientists and Laboratory Technicians showing the highest satisfaction scores.  
6.Attrition Rate by Gender and Age Group:
Highest attrition rates occur in the 25-34 age group (33.83% for males and 15.04% for females).

Filters & Interactions          

Education Filter: Allows users to view attrition based on the selected educational background.  
Use the education filter to analyze attrition trends based on educational qualifications.
